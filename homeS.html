What is an INC grade? An INC grade is a grade given to a student which indicates that the student has not completed all the assigned coursework for a class. The reason that a student may recieve an INC grade is if an instructor believes that the student can pass the course by completing the missing assignments. The problem is that students may find it difficult to manage and keep up with coursework. This can range from several factors from personal to blatant procrastination. Furthermore, students know very little about the BMCC grading system other than the A,B,C,D,F grades. Failure to understand the INC grade may lead to an unresolved INC grade. This lack o awareness and proactive engagement adversely affects student's academic progess and overall experience. The curreny INC resolution rate is only 50%.
